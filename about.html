About Us

Welcome to Jerin Caters & Events

Jerin Caters & Events is a Kerala-based company with over 24 years of experience in the catering and event management industry. From intimate family gatherings to grand corporate events, we take pride in delivering personalized services tailored to your vision. Our mission is to make your special occasions unforgettable by blending creativity, expertise, and unparalleled attention to detail.

we began with a passion for food and a commitment to quality service. Over the past two decades, we’ve evolved into a full-service event management company, handling everything from exquisite catering to meticulous event planning. Whether you’re hosting a wedding, corporate function, or a private celebration, we ensure a seamless experience that leaves a lasting impression on your guests.
